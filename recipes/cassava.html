Cassava Cake

Even I want to eat some. :(

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs packs grated cassava
  • 2 cups coconut milk
  • 1/2 12 oz. can evaporated milk
  • 3 pieces egg
  • 1/4 cup butter melted
  • 6 tbsp. cheddar cheese grated
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk
  • 14 tbsp. granulated white sugar
Topping Ingredients
  • 2 tbsp. flour
  • 2 tbsp. sugar
  • 1/2 cup condensed milk
  • 2 tbsp. cheddar cheese grated
  • 2 cups coconut milk

Instructions

  1. Combine grated cassava, butter, 1/2 cup condensed milk, 1/2 cup evaporated milk, 6 tbsp. cheddar cheese, 14 tbsp. sugar, and 2 eggs in a mixing bowl.
  2. Mix thoroughly.
  3. Add 2 cups coconut milk. Mix again.
  4. Grease the baking tray and pour in the mixed ingredients.
  5. Preheat to 350F for 10 minutes then put in the batter and bake for 1 hour.
  6. Combine 2 tbsp. sugar and flour in heated saucepan.
  7. Pour in 1/2 cup condensed milk then mix well.
  8. Add 2 tbsp. cheddar cheese while stirring.
  9. Pour 2 cups of coconut milk and stir for 10 minutes.
  10. Pour the topping over the Cassava Cake and spread evenly.
  11. Separate the yolk and egg white of the remaining egg.
  12. Glaze the topping with the egg white using a brush.
  13. Set oven to broil mode. Broil until color turns light brown.
  14. Garnish with extra grated cheese on top. Serve and enjoy!
