No-Bake Lasagna

Real no-bake lasagna! Garfield's and your favorite!

Ingredients

For lasagna:
  • 9 pieces lasagna
  • 3/4 cup cheddar cheese (shredded)
  • Water for boiling lasagna
For meat sauce:
  • 3/4 lb ground pork
  • 3 pieces hotdog sliced
  • 15 ounces tomato sauce
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 piece onion chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic minced
  • 3 tbsp. cooking oil
  • 1 tbsp. sugar
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste
White sauce:
  • 1/4 cup salted butter
  • 2 cups fresh milk
  • 3 1/2 tbsp. all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cheddar cheese shredded

Instructions

Making the meat sauce:
  1. Heat oil in a cooking pot.
  2. Saute onion and garlic until onion softens.
  3. Add ground pork. Cook until it turns light brown.
  4. Add hotdog and saute with the rest for one minute.
  5. Pour tomato sauce and water. Boil. Cover and cook between low to medium heat for 40 minutes.
  6. Add sugar and season with ground black pepper and salt. Set aside.
Making the lasagna:
  1. Boil 1 1/4 pint water.
  2. Add 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  3. Put lasagna in boiling water and continue to boil per package instructions.
  4. Drain the water. Set lasagna aside.
Making the white sauce:
  1. Melt butter in saucepan. Add half of the flour. Stir until blended.
  2. Add remaining flour and continue to stir. Gradually pour milk while stirring until sauce thickens.
  3. Add 1/2 cup cheddar cheese. Stir until melted.
Assembling the lasagna:
  1. Spread meat sauce on an 8x8 baking pan.
  2. Layer the lasagna and spread meat sauce over the lasagna.
  3. Spread the white sauce and sprinkle some shredded cheese on top.
  4. Arrange the second layer with the same order.
  5. Arrange the last layer. Top with with sauce first then meat sauce. Sprinkle cheese all over.
  6. Cover the pan with aluminum foil. Make sure that the foil does not touch the lasagna toppings.
  7. Place in turbo broiler in 400F for 35 minutes.
  8. Remove the foil. Set to 350F and continue to broil for 12 minutes.
  9. Remove from broiler. Slice and serve.
