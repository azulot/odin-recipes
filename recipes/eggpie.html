Egg Pie

Some real good yummy protein source!

Ingredients

Crust:
  • 2 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/2 cup cold water
  • 2 tbsp. granulated sugar
Filling
  • 1 3/4 cups evaporated milk
  • 3 pieces raw eggs
  • 1 piece egg (white separated from yolk)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup granulated sugar

Instructions

Crust:
  1. Mix flour, sugar, and salt.
  2. Add the butter and mix.
  3. Gradually pour cold water while mixing. Mix well.
  4. Gather dough and mold into ball-shape.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
  6. Sprinkle flour on a clean flat surface and flatten dough using a rolling pin. Make wide enough for desired pie size.
  7. Arrange flattened dough on top of baking pan and cut extra edges using scissors.
  8. Refrigerate.
Filling:
  1. Heat the evaporated milk in the microwave for 2 minutes.
  2. Combine 3 raw eggs and egg yolk then mix.
  3. Gradually add granulated sugar while whisking.
  4. Put in vanilla extract. Mix well.
  5. Put in scalded milk. Mix well.
  6. Beat separated egg white until it forms soft peaks.
  7. Fold the process egg with in the milk-eggs-sugar mixture.
Combining crust and filling:
  1. Preheat oven to 350F.
  2. Pour the filling mixture on refrigerated pie crust.
  3. Bake for 15 minutes at 350F then lower to 325F and continue baking for 30 to 40 minutes.
  4. Remove from oven and let cool.
  5. Serve and enjoy!
